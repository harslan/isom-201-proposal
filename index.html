üîç Innovation/Inquiry

14-Week Course Structure

1

Week 1: AI Literacy for Business Leaders

What business professionals need to know about AI. Types of AI. Introduction to SAIL Framework.

2

Week 2: How AI Actually Works

Understanding AI conceptually (no coding required). The "black box" problem. Prompt engineering basics.

3

Week 3: Leadership - When to Use AI

Decision framework: Should we use AI for this? Ethical considerations. Stakeholder impact assessment.

4

Week 4: Innovation & Social Intelligence

Critical questioning of AI outputs. Communicating to non-technical stakeholders. Exam 1.

5-13

Weeks 5-13: Major-Specific Projects

Students work in domain teams on AI projects relevant to their major. All projects assessed using SAIL rubric.

14

Week 14: Cross-Functional Integration

Project presentations. Learning from other domains. Reflection on transfer learning.
